#+title: Alignment


* When to use
Remember these rules when you align with Elm-UI:

** Patterns
*** <icon> <text>  ... <badge>
#+begin_src elm

Ui.row [ Ui.wrap, Ui.width Ui.fill, ... ]
    [ -- can also work with Ui.el [ Ui.alignLeft ] ...
      PhosphorIcon.<icon> [ PhosphorIcon.alignLeft ]
    , Ui.el [ Ui.alignLeft ] <| textEl
    , -- Works with .row or .column with
      Ui.el [ Ui.alignRight ] <| badgeEl
    ]
#+end_src
